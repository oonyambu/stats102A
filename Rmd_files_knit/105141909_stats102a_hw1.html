Problem 1

gcd:
  1.Set x as the smaller integer of the two inputs
2.Set y as the larger integer of the two inputs
3.WHILE the remainder of x divided by y is not 0
3.1 Store y value in another temporary variable
3.2 Change y value to be the the remainder of x divided by y
3.3 Change x value to the original y which we store in the temporary variable
4.Return y

gcd_3:
1.Calculate the greatest common divisor of b and c
2.Store the above result in a variable
3.Calculate the greatest common divisor of a and the greatest common divisor of b and c
4.return result

get_factors:
1.Set factors as an empty integer variable
2.Set i = 2
3.WHILE (x is not a prime number)
3.1 IF (i is a prime number)
3.1.1 IF (x is divisible by i)
3.1.1.1 Put i into factors
3.1.1.2 Change x to x/i
3.1.1.3 Change i to 2
3.1.2 ELSE Add 1 on i
3.2 ELSE Add 1 on i
4.Put x into factors
5.Return factors
